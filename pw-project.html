    • What is Preference Wear?

    A platform that promotes the best quality clothing sustainable fabrics for people with skin conditions such as eczema.
    This app is also usable for people without skin conditions. its useful if you want to find clothing styles for different seasons or any type of sustainable clothing from any brand, all in one place.

    What problems does it solve?

    • Allow people with skin condition(s) to easily find clothing fabrics that doesn't irritate their skin.
    • For people that are looking for specific fabric(s), it reduces the time it takes to find those fabrics.
    • Eliminate the need to have an e-commerce website if a brand sells quality clothing products.
